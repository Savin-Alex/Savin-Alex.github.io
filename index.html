About

From industrial design to AI delivery.

I trained as an IT engineer — a degree in Information Systems — then led design-and-engineering projects for industrial facilities for five years: the largest at ₽130M+, teams up to 15, five-plus contractors, hard deadlines and shifting stakeholders. I shipped on time through weekly risk audits and fast onboarding of new decision-makers, and I built the change-control and stakeholder processes that kept scope honest.

Now I bring that delivery discipline to AI. I treat GenAI as a controlled process: requirements and acceptance criteria, backlogs, quality gates, risk registers, and evidence packages — so an experiment becomes something a team can audit, trust, and ship. My technical background (Python, APIs, LLMs) means I speak the same language as the engineers I work with.
